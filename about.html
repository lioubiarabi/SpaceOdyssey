About

AstroTech Studio: Inspiring space learning through design and code

AstroTech Studio est un studio spécialisé dans la création de plateformes éducatives dédiées à l’espace et aux sciences, alliant design soigné, rigueur pédagogique et innovation technologique. Sa mission est de rendre l’astronomie accessible et inspirante pour les jeunes publics en proposant des expériences claires, visuelles et engageantes, tout en valorisant l’esprit critique et la curiosité scientifique.F

How We Work

The team follows a research-informed process that balances scientific rigor with delightful design. Accessibility, performance, and responsiveness guide every decision so learners can enjoy the site on any device and connection speed.

Curiosity

Encourage questions and exploration by inviting “why/what if” prompts in reviews and dedicating time for mini-experiments and design spikes each sprint.

Accuracy

Cite credible data and keep facts current by maintaining a source log of all astronomy facts and images, using versioned content snippets, and scheduling periodic content audits.

Accessibility

Design for inclusive, barrier-free learning by applying semantic HTML, clear focus states, sufficient color contrast, and descriptive alt text.

Creativity

Use visuals and stories to deepen understanding through narrative micro-copy, illustrative diagrams, and subtle motion that reinforces meaning.
